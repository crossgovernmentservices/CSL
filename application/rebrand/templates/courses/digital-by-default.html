Digital awareness: ‘digital by default’

Description

Explaining what ‘digital by default’ means for government and civil servants, this course explores the importance of moving services online for you, your department and the citizen.

This course uses case studies from across government to demonstrate the different ways government is digitally transforming its services by making them more effective and accessible for customers.

Learning outcomes

At the end of this course, you will be able to:

  • explain what ‘digital’ means in a government context
  • explain why effective digital services are important
  • understand the government digital strategy
