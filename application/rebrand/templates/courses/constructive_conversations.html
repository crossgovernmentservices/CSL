Pre-learning requirements

You’ll need to have completed the:

  • Managing difficult behaviour e-learning
  • Managing performance e-learning
  • Giving and receiving feedback crib guide

You’ll also need to come along with an example of a real-life work situation for a practice session in the workshop.

Make sure you have discussed your individual learning objectives with your line manager and agreed follow-up activities to help you use and embed the new skills you'll learn.

You’ll find plenty of advice on how to get the most out of this learning event in our guide to learning in the workplace.
