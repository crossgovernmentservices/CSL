Payment options

What are the different payment options?

There are three ways to pay for your learning:

  1. Purchase order - you will need to have a valid purchase order number from your finance team to complete this booking.
  2. Government procurement card - your card will be debited as soon as the booking is completed. Personal credit cards cannot be used.
  3. Financial approval - when prompted please select an approver to confirm your booking. To find out whether your organisation uses the financial approver process, please contact your learning and development team. They should be able to confirm the name of your financial approver.
